## Body

<p>Find out if your email will hit the inbox, or the dreaded spam folder across all of todays leading email providers including Gmail, Hotmail and more. If there are any issues with your email content, your infrastructure or your reputation, we'll let you know how to fix them.</p>
<p>Featuring:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Inbox Placement<br /></strong>Automatically inbox test and spam test your email across the worlds leadings inbox providers.<br /><br /></li>
<li><strong>Content Analysis<br /></strong>Check your subject, email body or links aren't causing non delivery of your emails.<br /><br /></li>
<li><strong>Server Reputation<br /></strong>Find out if your email server is blacklisted or performing badly with the top ESP's.<br /><br /></li>
<li><strong>Sender Reputation<br /></strong>Check your sending email address domain is performing as it should be.<br /><br /></li>
<li><strong>Email Timing<br /></strong>See how long it takes for your email to go from outbox to inbox.<br /><br /></li>
<li><strong>Authentication Testing<br /></strong>Is your email properly authenticated? Make sure your SPF, DKIM and DMARC settings are all working correctly.</li>
</ul>
